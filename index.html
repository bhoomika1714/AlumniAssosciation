About Us

Our Alumni Association aims to foster a lifelong relationship between alumni and the institution. Founded in 1985, we have been supporting our graduates for over 40 years.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant, engaged alumni community that supports both the institution and its graduates throughout their lives and careers.

Our Vision

To be the most active and supportive alumni network, creating value for all members and contributing to the continued success of our institution.

Our Values

Connection, Support, Excellence, Innovation, and Lifelong Learning are the core values that guide all of our initiatives and programs.
